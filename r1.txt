Soak 1/2 cup stale bread cubes in 1/2 cup milk; 
mix with 8 beaten eggs. Add 3/4 cup ricotta, 
1 teaspoon chopped thyme, and salt and pepper. 
Saute 1 cup shredded zucchini in an ovenproof 
nonstick skillet with oil. 
Add the egg mixture; 

cook until the bottom sets, then bake at 325 degrees F, 25 minutes.
